Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

CK Tools ("we", "us", or "our") operates the FloatNet and WheelVolume mobile applications (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do NOT collect any personally identifiable information (PII). Our apps are designed to work locally on your device.

  • FloatNet (NetworkMonitor): Uses network statistics permissions to display your data usage in real-time. This data never leaves your device.
  • WheelVolume: Uses accessibility/overlay permissions to provide volume control functionality. No input or data is recorded or transmitted.

2. Permissions and Their Purpose

Our apps require certain system permissions to function. We only request permissions that are absolutely necessary for the core features:

  • Usage Access: Required by FloatNet to identify which apps are consuming bandwidth.
  • Display Over Other Apps: Required by both apps to show floating windows or overlays.
  • Modify System Settings: Required by WheelVolume to adjust the device's volume levels.

3. Third-Party Services

Our Service may include advertisements (AdMob) or in-app purchases (Google Play Billing). These third-party services may collect information used to identify you according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not store or retain any of your information on our servers.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
